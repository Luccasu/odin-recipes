Lasagna

Ingredients

  • Lean ground beef
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Minced onions
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Cook the lasagna noodles.
  2. Season the beef with salt and pepper.
  3. Fry the beef with minced onions.
  4. Add tomato sauce.
  5. Layer the noodle with the fried beef.
